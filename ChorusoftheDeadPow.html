Chorus

Song of the dead, marching in unison through creation. Its rhythm drowning the voices of hope and despair.

Tier 1: Melody

Humming to the music of the Deceased Whenever you score a killing blow against a target, score a critical hit on an attack, or a target critically fails against a saving throw of yours that deals single target damage, your compulsion forces you to hum to the rhythm of the dead, until the end of your next turn. While humming the rhythm all damage dealt to you is delayed, until the humming ends. The damage after is dealt in a single blow, but cannot kill you.

Tier 4: Consumption of the Chorus

Rotting Husk of a walking nightmare Your body finally feels the overwhelming effects of the decay, the Chorus that you willingly took upon yourself creeping ever so deeper into your essence. You only have one failed death save on your character, you are able to perform weapon attacks and move at half speed while at 0 or less HP, puppeted by the chorus, but once you die you lose 1d6→2d6→3d6 Wisdom. Once your wisdom score hits -30, you lose all of yourself and merge with the chorus.
